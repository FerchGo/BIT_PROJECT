Colombian Nature Reserves

Abstract

Colombia is a country with a great variety of climates and ecosystems, which has allowed the development of a great diversity of flora and fauna. The country has 59 natural areas protected by the state, which represent 12.2% of the national territory. These areas are divided into 56 natural national parks, 2 national natural reserves and 1 national natural sanctuary. The national parks are divided into 43 continental parks, 12 marine parks and 1 fluvial park. The national natural reserves are divided into 1 continental reserve and 1 marine reserve. The national natural sanctuary is a marine sanctuary.
